How a garment is born 

Time, skill, and creativity: these are some of the elements that make it possible to transform a fabric into a finished garment.

 

Pattern Making 

 

Time after time we achieve outstanding results in the look, feel and fit of our tailor made suits. 

 

Your body shape doesn't matter we achieve the highest quality fit that any client of ours could dream of. However, it takes even more experience to perfect the pattern-making process. Each person that walks through our doors is a different shape and unique in their way, not to mention the requirements in fit are completely different for each person. No one size fits all.  

 

We determine your body shape and measurements. Then we feed this information into our software. After which we select your desired fabric and style that matches your preferences. Once all this is stored in our system. Your Suit is now put under production. The Fabric is cut accurately through our advanced cut technology leaving no room for errors. Now, your suit moves through our production line adding small and exquisite details.  

 

when purchasing more garments from us, it becomes much quicker and easier as we have already perfected your fit through the process, and we now store your very own pattern with all the curves ingrained in your pattern for any future orders. The relationship you build with us over a lifetime is like no other especially once your pattern is perfected.

 

Cut & Sew 

 

Once the fabric is precisely cut and trimmed according to your body shape and measurements. It's time now to fix the Pockets, the canvas and handpicked lining of your choice are applied, and buttons are selected according to your choice. From here the final sewing of your suits starts.

 

Floating Canvas 

 

The canvas is cut to the jacket's shape, then the wool is stitched to the canvas. Over time, as you wear the jacket, the canvas conforms to your body's shape, creating an excellent fit. The canvas lining allows the suit fabric to drape naturally, allowing a clean, well-put-together look.

 

The foundation of all Brown Bear Bespoke jackets is the canvas—a breathable, natural blend of cotton, horsehair, and mohair that gives your jacket its shape. The canvas is a symbol of quality and the term ‘floating’ comes from the fact that the canvas is sewn - of course, it takes 3 times longer but worth the effort, rather than glued or fused into the arm holes and lapel, enabling the canvas to ‘float’ on the body, while the outer fabric is free to move elegantly and fluidly. Choose from our traditional half weight canvas, full canvas Fused canvas, or no canvassing at all depending on the style, occasion, and climate.

 

Shoulder Pads 

 

Our collection encompasses a diverse range of shoulder pad styles including straight cut, drop style, stiffener, and half-moon designs, each available in various sizes to complement your unique shoulder shape and body posture. Crafted from traditional soft, premium materials such as natural fibers and eco-friendly foam, our shoulder pads are designed for maximum comfort, flexibility, and durability. Our attention to the jacket's construction ensures a natural and relaxed shoulder silhouette, emphasizing the garment's overall elegance and comfort.

 

Lining 

 

The interior lining of a suit is just as crucial as its exterior fabric. To ensure superior quality, we provide an exceptional selection of linings made from top-tier Bemberg, Viscose, Cupro, and Silk. Crafted from the finest materials, our linings offer a silky-smooth texture, unparalleled softness, and are highly effective in absorbing and releasing moisture, enhancing your comfort. Choosing the right lining not only elevates your suit's feel and appearance but also plays a pivotal role in prolonging the lifespan of your garment.

 

Buttons & Thread 

 

We empower you to personalize your suit with buttons that reflect your style. Our collection features exquisite Bull-Horn, Mother of Pearl, and Corozo buttons, renowned for their durability and ability to enhance the visual appeal of your suit. Each button is meticulously hand-sewn using threads of the highest quality, available in cotton, silk, or waxed silamide. This careful process adds a touch of finesse and ensures the longevity of the buttons, safeguarding them from loosening or detaching over time.

Each garment is adorned with our exclusive brand label, alongside branded fabric labels, as a mark of authenticity and craftsmanship. This emblem represents the pride and dedication we invest in every brown bear bespoke product. It's not just a label; it's a symbol of the trust and connection shared between us, and a testament to your discerning choice in brand.

We take pride and care in every detail of your garment and recognise that the little details are just as paramount—every step of the way, we place an emphasis on quality control.Throughout, hand made process provide our customer a creation space for their own garments. Hand sewn angled hem 

 

Something you might hardly recognize but can only be found in bespoke garments is hand sewn angled hem. Angled hem will help the trousers falls nicely on your dress shoes and and look more slick than un-angled trousers hem. Now you know why a pair of trousers looked more dapper on you than the others, it thanks to craftsmanship.

 

Symmetric pattern

 

Checked jackets and patterned fabrics require more careful evaluation and greater craftsmanship in order to obtain garments that bring out the best of the pattern imprinted on the fabric, with perfectly matching and well-defined lines, impossible to reproduce in an industrial process.
