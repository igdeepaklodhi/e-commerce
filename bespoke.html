STRICTLY BY HAND

IT'S THE DETAILS THAT MAKE THE DIFFERENCE

Merging premium Italian raw materials with the unparalleled expertise of artisans devoted to their distinguished craft, we produce tailor-made pieces that not only transcend the pace of changing trends but also offer a durability far surpassing that of mass-produced suits.

beauty and tradition

THE CHOICE OF MATERIALS

In the recipe for making an excellent tailor-made suit, one of the most important ingredients is certainly the fabric used. For this reason we select fabrics from the most prestigious textile houses, innovative yarns that masterfully combine tradition, technology, beauty and performance. The choice of fabric is individual and subjective, it is not taken for granted that a certain fabric will suit everyone, and no one else better than your tailor can know it.

luxury and innovation

THE FABRICS

Our exquisite fabrics are procured from the most esteemed mills located in Northern Italy's Biella region, including renowned establishments like Beste and Vitale Barberis Canonico. These Italian powerhouses, with their centuries-old traditions and unparalleled craftsmanship, have become emblematic of luxury, innovation, and quality in the textile industry. We offer a variety of super luxurious fabrics from renowned textile manufacturers around the globe.

perfection and elegance

STRUCTURE

When it comes to jackets, the inside is just as important as the outside. A good jacket does not take on a fluid shape and naturally rounds, it needs an inner layer that gives it a curved shape and a rich structure. In homage to the craftsmanship of the old Italian school, to line the inside of the suits we use a thin layer of cotton reinforced with horsehair. The canvas adapts to the shape of the body, for a perfect fit and an elegant look.
