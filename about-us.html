We Tailor Exclusively to your Specifications

Bespoke is an art which over the years has been limited to very formal or Official work wear & a lot many do not experience this satisfying process of getting measured & wearing a garment that meant for you alone.

The idea behind Brown Bear is to take this art to everyone & create this experience for all of us. We have created an entire line from professional clothing to casual clothing which is fit for all, whether you work from office or home.

We create anything from a suit to a overcoat or Shirt, Denims, Polos. We have a wide range of products for you to select from.

Brown Bear tailor company is an initiative to achieve sustainable luxury fashion through our responsible choice of materials &. Efforts to create a healthier future for our Planet. With no compromise on quality & craftmanship.

Jai Mehtani

Our Philosophy

We are pioneers at everything we do, and we work hard to keep it that way. It's Time to. Fashion for Earth

They say you can have anything you want, if you dress for it. We want a more sustainable future for the planet.”

At Brown Bear we realize our responsibilities towards the planet. Ergo all materials, accessories & trims are handpicked to reflect highest quality & also sustainability such as horse hair interlining, wool collar felts, pure materials like wool, cotton, linen, without any polyester, and much more.

Brown Bear Tailoring

Brown Bear Tailoring provides the superior fit and quality of a bespoke suit, without the long wait and multiple fittings.

Efficient Measurement

With an efficient measurement matrix and a combination of handmade and mechanical techniques, we can deliver the perfectly styled & fitted suit in just 4-5 weeks.

Consistency & Accuracy

Maintaining consistency & accuracy is one of our highest priorities. To cater to this all the patterns are saved on file so you can have the exact same fit and ease for all future orders.
